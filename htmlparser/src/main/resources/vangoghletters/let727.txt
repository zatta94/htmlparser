Title| Arnold Koning to Vincent van Gogh. Winschoten, Monday, 31 December 1888. - Vincent van Gogh Letters
Location|Winschoten
Date|Monday, 31 December 1888
To|Vincent van Gogh
From|Arnold Koning
Url letter|http://vangoghletters.org/vg/letters/let727/letter.html
Letter|Winschoten 31 December 1888
My dear friend!
You have heard nothing from me for a long time . Now I wish you herewith a happy New Year , and will shortly reply to your letter that I received this summer .
Adieu , more soon
A . H . Koning
